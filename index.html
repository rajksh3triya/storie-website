The Design Journey

A comprehensive, step-by-step masterplan from conceptualization to finishing touches.

01

Vision & Consultation

A personal meeting to define your functional needs, aesthetic inspirations, and architectural potential of the canvas.

02

Concept & Materiality

Drafting spatial layouts, architectural sketching, and establishing a tailored physical mood board of marbles, linen, and metals.

03

Technical Architecture

Developing full 3D visual renderings and comprehensive electrical, millwork, and lighting layouts for contractors.

04

Artisanal Curation

Procuring bespoke furniture, handcrafted light installations, and commissioning custom details from master workshops globally.

05

Styling & Reveal

On-site staging, art hanging, layering texture, and finishing styling coordinates to deliver your fully completed STORIE.
